Distribution Experiments

If you don't let the labrats plugin calculate and distribute your user's into test buckets (by not specifying a key), then you need a good hashing function to convert your "id" into a number.

The one built into the plugin is pretty simplistic, and the first two experiments demonstrate this. The second uses the Murmur Hash algorithm, and as you can see, is a bit better at distributing GUIDs into test buckets.
